## We become part of your event, we don't just host it.

Peel Manor House was specially built as a bed and breakfast to offer a luxury getaway for people who wanted to escape the hustle and bustle of Perth.

But when we opened in 2000, word of this idyllic retreat quickly spread, and before we knew it, people were travelling a fair distance to host their events and special occasions here at the manor.

As requests increased, it was only natural that we took the opportunity to share the manor with the rest of the world. We soon opened the grounds for weddings, and the large rooms for conferences and functions.

Today, Peel Manor House is widely regarded as one of Western Australia's premier function and accommodation venues. Due to its impressive semi-rural setting and unique all-in-one service, the manor is now a favourite for weddings. It is recommended by the many brides we've helped in turning their dream weddings into reality.

Peel Manor sits on 10.5 acres 40 minute south of Perth and under 20 minutes north of Mandurah. It's nestled amongst magnificent Tuart Trees and surrounded by superb gardens and a peaceful lake. We also have a secret garden complete with its own real Pinnacles that are over 60,000 years old.

Our guests can visit local farms for some fresh produce, and go to the six wineries that are a short drive from us.

The Georgian-style mansion, which is owned and operated by a local family, features a sweeping staircase, 14 beautiful king-sized deluxe suites (some with double-spa baths), a full-sized billiards table and baby grand piano for you to enjoy.
