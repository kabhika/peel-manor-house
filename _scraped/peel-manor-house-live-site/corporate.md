## Everything You Need For Your Corporate Event

There's nothing quite like Peel Manor House. If you're looking to host an event and want to work with team who focuses attention on the finest detail, you'll be impressed with what you can achieve at our manor.

##### Inspire your guests with our laid-back yet sophisticated setting.

We're not too far from the city. It takes only a short 40-minute drive from Perth and 20 minutes from Mandurah. Our beautiful Georgian-style manor is spacious enough for large events and is the ideal setting for any type of event for more than 150 guests.

Whether it's a conference, seminar, fundraiser, team building activity or a charity ball, we have room for you. If you need outdoor function space, you'll have 10.5 acres of garden space to play in.
