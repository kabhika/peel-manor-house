## Would you like to experience some Old School Charm?

High Tea is an old school oasis in the modern age. Known by the iconic three tier serving platter of delectable sweet and savoury items.

We invite you to come and enjoy at high tea in the gorgeous surrounds of an old english manor on 10 1/2 acres, just a short drive south of Perth.

Peel Manor House is the winner of the 2016 Catering Institute Gold Plate Award for High Tea!

Our High Teas can be booked for a minimum of 2 people or for a celebration of 100.

See our events page/Book Now button for available dates or if you have a milestone occasion to celebrate, contact us for an alternative date. (subject to terms and availability)

We can also cater for dietary requirements of Gluten-Free, Diary-Free, Lactose Intolerant, Vegetarian and Vegan, please advise of your dietary requirements when booking.

(Surcharges apply for Gluten-Free, Diary-Free, Lactose Intolerant and Vegan)

Children are most welcome at High Teas, please ensure children remain seated at their table for their safety and as courtesy to other patrons, should you require a high chair or room for a pram, please advise us when booking.

We also welcome tweens and teens high tea birthday celebrations.

We even host Doggie High Teas! - yes you can bring pooch along and have a high tea on the grounds with your four legged friend.

For all High Tea bookings we require payment in full. Your payment is non-refundable, but is wholly transferrable to another date.

Subject to availability this is provided that cancellation occurs no later than five (5) days prior to your reservation, cancellation must be made by calling 08 9524 2838.
