## Looking for stunning accommodation outside of Perth?

**Peel Manor House** is a stunning Georgian-style manor situated on beautiful semi-rural surrounds, only **40 minutes south of Perth.**

The manor features 10 luxuriously appointed king suites and 4 Spa Suites which include the Deluxe, Executive, Residence & Presidential Suite. Each room commands breathtaking views of our idyllic lake and beautiful landscaped gardens.

Each of our suites are of various configurations from the king bed for ultimate comfort to family suites of a King and two single beds or twin suites of two Queen beds, a sitting area, en-suite facilities, 42" television, and tea & coffee making facilities, ironing board and iron and hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. Guests also enjoy a full English breakfast each morning.

You and your loved one or guests can stroll the 10 ½ acres, go to our Manor Bar for a drink, or make use of our full-sized billiards table and baby grand piano, or you may wish to explore the nearby wineries and local fresh produce.

Whether you're after a weekend getaway, or planning a special event, Peel Manor House is the perfect venue for those who enjoy privacy, luxury and superior hospitality.
